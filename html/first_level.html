First level - Tutorial

Welcome to the first level tutorial.
The first level is simple and does not have any major functionalities. As the levels progress, their difficulty increases.
In this level there are cards that are randomly obtained. Each hero carries a certain number of points (3,5 and 7), while the other cards have slightly different meanings. On the link you can see all the cards in the game as well as their meanings. It is not possible to move to the next level until the player has successfully won the first level.
Thanks for playing PyRoyale.
